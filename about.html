Company History

Launched in 2019, Solomon E-Shopping is the number-one online shopping and selling destination in Southeast Asia – present in Indonesia, Malaysia, the Philippines, Singapore, Thailand and Vietnam. As the pioneer of the eCommerce ecosystem in the Southeast Asia, Solomon E-Shopping has 155,000 sellers and 3,000 brands serving 560 million consumers in the region through its marketplace platform, supported by a wide range of tailored marketing, data and service solutions. With 300 million SKUs available, Solomon E-Shopping offers the widest range of products in categories from consumer electronics to household goods. Focused on delivering an excellent customer experience, it offers multiple payment methods including cash-on-delivery, comprehensive customer care and hassle-free returns through its own first and last mile delivery arm supported by approximately 100 logistics partners. Solomon E-Shopping Group is majority owned by Steffen Joshua Solomon
